How the Chapter Builds Its Case

Beyond the Market
Options do not exist in a vacuum. Traders face 'meta risks'‚Äîexternal factors like legal changes, government intervention, and moral hazards.
2
Currency and Inflation
All trades are settled in fiat currency. Hyperinflation or sudden devaluations can wipe out 'real' returns even if the trading strategy is successful.
3
Theft and Fraud
The history of finance is littered with rogue traders and Ponzi schemes. Due diligence on counterparties and custodial oversight is non-negotiable.
4
Counterparty Reliability
In a crisis, the institution on the other side of your trade might not be able to pay. Diversifying across brokers and clearinghouses is essential.
