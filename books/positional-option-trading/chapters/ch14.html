Argument Flow

How the Chapter Builds Its Case

1
The Cost of Doing Business
Transaction costs (commissions, spreads, and market impact) can turn a profitable strategy into a losing one. Execution is where alpha is often lost.
2
The Execution Dilemma
Traders must balance being 'aggressive' (getting the fill) vs. 'passive' (minimizing the spread). The choice depends on the expected speed of the market move.
3
Market Impact
Large orders move the price. Understanding 'temporary' vs. 'permanent' impact is crucial for institutional-sized traders.
4
Algorithmic Benchmarks
Using VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price) helps minimize impact by participating in the market's natural flow rather than fighting against it.
